Why Invicta

Retail prices are broken. We fix that.

Invicta Home Supply started with a simple observation: brand new flooring, appliances, and renovation supplies get liquidated every week at a fraction of shelf price — overstock, discontinued runs, closeout lots, floor samples. Most of it never makes it to a customer who'd actually want it. None of it is used or open-box — it's new stock that retailers are clearing out.

We buy those lots, inspect every item ourselves, and only list what's genuinely brand new. No warehouse markup, no shipping fees, no guessing what you're getting — you see it, you reserve it, you pick it up.

Every item is sold locally, in person. That keeps our costs low and lets us price things honestly.
