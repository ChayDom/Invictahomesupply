Frequently Asked

Do you ship items?

No — everything is local pickup only. This keeps our prices low and lets you inspect items in person before paying.

Do I need to pay online to reserve?

No online payment required. A text or call holds your item for 24 hours; you pay when you pick it up.

What payment do you accept at pickup?

Cash, card, or Venmo — whatever's easiest for you.

How long is an item held?

24 hours from when you reserve it. After that it goes back up for grabs.
